Media manager · internal

Carousels & media across the site

Each card below is a media slot live on the site. Add image URLs, video links or YouTube/Vimeo links. Save and the slot updates everywhere.

How to use this

1 · Each slot maps to a <div class="media-slot" data-slot="..."> placeholder somewhere on the site.

2 · Paste any image or video URL — Cloudinary, S3, the orionmis.co.uk CDN, YouTube, Vimeo, or a direct .mp4.

3 · Hit Export media.json, copy the output, replace the contents of media.json in the repo and push. The carousel widget will pick it up automatically on the next page load.

4 · Changes you make here are saved to your browser as a draft. They become live only after you replace media.json in the repo.
