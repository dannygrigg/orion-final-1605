FAQs

Sortation finance, asked directly.

Questions parcel centres and 3PLs ask about financing a sortation system.

Can sortation systems be financed?

Yes. Helix parcel sortation systems can be structured around finance options, subject to specification and approval, helping parcel centres and 3PLs reduce manual sorting without waiting for a large CAPEX cycle.

How much does a Helix system cost?

System cost depends on throughput, destinations, parcel profile, WCS scope and install conditions. Real Orion projects have ranged from around £400k for 24 destinations to over £1.1m for 100+ destinations. The ROI engine gives a sized indicative figure based on your numbers.

What's the monthly finance figure for a Helix system?

Indicative monthly figures typically range from £4,000/month for entry-level systems on a 60-month finance term. Final figures depend on system spec, finance terms and approval. The ROI engine on the homepage gives a live indicative monthly figure.

How quickly can a Helix system be delivered?

Full Helix sortation systems typically run 12–25 weeks from order to live. Typical site install completes in under 4 weeks. Detailed timelines are confirmed in the Functional Design Specification stage.

Is WCS included in the finance case?

Yes. Orion WCS, dashboards, routing logic and alarming are included with every Helix system as standard — and form part of the financed equipment list.

Is finance guaranteed?

No. Finance is subject to specification, lender approval and commercial terms. Orion can help build the technical and commercial case — but we do not promise guaranteed approval.
