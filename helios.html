What Helios does

Eight modules. One live operation.

Not a dashboard bolted on after the fact — a full warehouse operating system running live today. Every module below is in production on real sites.

Home · command surface

A personal, drag-and-drop dashboard. Every user arranges their own live widgets — throughput, read rate, active sorters, routing, 365-day heatmaps — the way they want to see the site.

Parcels · live traceability

Every parcel, live: barcode, dimensions, weight, destination, chute, service and read status. Search any parcel, click through to its capture image — full traceability, per sorter or site-wide.

Stats · live operations

Real-time throughput, good / multi / no-read rates, uptime and active sorters — site-wide or per sorter, from a 10-minute window to a full day, with a rolling 7-day heatmap and CSV export.

Reports · analytics & insight

Daily volume against target, shift breakdowns, sorter leaderboards, out-of-gauge and volumetric (m³) reports — with auto-generated insights on what changed, exported to CSV or PDF.

Chute routing

Assign depots to chutes in a few clicks and push the new config straight to the physical sorter through its edge agent — rebalance lanes for peak without a controls engineer on site.

SCADA · live digital twin

A real-time 3D model of your actual sorters and conveyors, parcels moving as they move on the floor — with active and historic alarms you can acknowledge without leaving the screen.

Engineering · schematics

The full EPLAN electrical schematics live in the platform — panel layouts, cable runs, PROFINET, power supplies, E-stops. The drawings sit next to the machine they describe, always current.

Maintenance · built-in CMMS

Raise work orders against any asset, run a Kanban board through to completion, schedule PMs and track spares with datasheets and reorder levels — planned maintenance instead of firefighting.

Ask Orion · the AI assistant Helios watches the data and surfaces what matters in plain English — "read rate drops 2–3% at the 14:00 shift change — lighting or cleaning?" · "chute 17 up 28% week-on-week, may need rebalancing." Insight, not just numbers.
