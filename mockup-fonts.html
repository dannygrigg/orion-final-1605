What changed vs the current site

01 · Typeface
Inter replaces DM Sans + DM Serif Display. One sans-serif family, all weights from 300 (light) to 900 (black). No more italic serif headlines — every headline is a heavy sans for that operations-dashboard feel.
02 · Headline weight
H1 is Inter 800 at clamp(46–84px), letter-spacing tight to -.035em. Cyan accent on the key phrase rather than italic. Mimics the HELIOS logo's bold black weight.
03 · Stat numbers
Big stats now use Inter 300 (light weight) instead of DM Serif. Same number, totally different feel — reads as dashboard data, not editorial.
04 · Mono labels
JetBrains Mono 600 with .16em letter-spacing for eyebrows and labels. Cyan accent colour shifts these from "decoration" to "system label", closer to the HELIOS dashboard.
05 · Body copy
Body is Inter 400 at 18px / 1.65 with slight negative letter-spacing. Reads more like a software interface than a brochure.
06 · CTA buttons
Buttons swap from weight 800 serif-adjacent to Inter 600 at 14px. Cleaner, more tech-product, less marketing-y.
07 · Feature list
Feature numbers are JetBrains Mono rather than DM Serif large-italic. Pulls everything towards "spec sheet" rather than "designed prose".
08 · KPI cards
Stat strip now reads as dashboard cards with cyan accent rails — directly echoing the HELIOS sidebar styling. Numbers in light weight, labels in heavy mono. Same data, dashboard frame.
