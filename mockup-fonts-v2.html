v2 · Inter body + serif italic accents

01 · Two typefaces, deliberate roles
Inter handles all plain text — body, headlines, labels, buttons. DM Serif Display italic ONLY shows up inside <em> tags as the cyan accent on key phrases. Two voices, clean handoff.
02 · Headline weight
H1 is Inter 600 (semibold) — not the heavy 800 from v1. Restrained, calm, doesn't overpower the surrounding sections.
03 · Italic accent
Words like warehouse intelligence, Protect profit, real freight stay in DM Serif Display italic + cyan — same emphasis pattern as the current live index, gives Orion its signature voice.
04 · Mono labels unchanged
Eyebrows and stat labels still JetBrains Mono, cyan, wide letter-spacing. They anchor the dashboard feel.
05 · Stat values
Big numbers in Inter 400 (regular). No italic, no serif on the numbers themselves — they read as data, not display type.
06 · Why this works
Sans-serif body keeps the page calm and modern. Serif italic adds just enough personality on the words that matter. Best of both — refined like v1, with Orion's voice intact.
