Scan, Divert & Convey System
Dwg NoORI-SS-001
RevA
Date23 / 05 / 2025
Plan View (Top)

Description

This system conveys products through a scan tunnel and diverts them left, right, or straight using Helix units.

Components

  • Top Roller Conveyor: Customer existing conveyor
  • 2 × Gap Conveyors (1610mm): Infeed and outfeed of scan tunnel
  • Scan Tunnel: KEYENCE SR500 (shown in cyan)
  • Helix Diverters (3 units): Left, Right & Straight
  • Straight Through Conveyor (1610mm): Straight ahead path

Conveyor details

  • Conveyor Width (Belt): 1050mm
  • Conveyor Lengths:
      – Gap Conveyors: 1610mm
      – Straight Conveyor: 1610mm
  • Overall Length (approx): 5812.54mm
  • Left & Right Diverter Length (incl. angle): 1605mm
  • Diverter Zone Length (straight section before split): 591.8mm

Keyence SR500 (multiple installed)

  • 3 × SR500 units shown in cyan (infeed, scan tunnel, outfeed)

Top to bottom
